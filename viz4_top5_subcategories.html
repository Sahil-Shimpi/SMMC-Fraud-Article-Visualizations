Top 5 Fraud Subcategories in Illinois, 2025
Sorted by total reported dollar loss. The categories generating the most reports are not the ones causing the most financial damage.
Investment fraud alone accounts for nearly half of all Illinois fraud losses. The Miscellaneous Investments & Investment Advice category totals $175.9M — more than the remaining four categories combined — despite having far fewer reports than government or business imposter scams.
